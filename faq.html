How do I get an appointment with you?
When my books open, please fill out MY booking form TO REQUEST AN APPOINTMENT.  I'm currently booking appointments 1-2 months out at a time. Follow me on Instagram for updates & last minute availability @cece.pokes
What is your pricing like?
My hourly rate is $200/hr with a $200 minimum for flash and a $250 minimum for custom work.  Most of my pieces range from $200-500.  I'll work with your budget for larger pieces !  All small/medium pieces will be subject to my hourly rate & I'm happy to provide an estimate. 
When do you schedule appointments?
I tattoo on Tuesdays, Thursdays, Fridays, & Saturdays, 12-7pm.  I generally book my appointments for 12 and 3pm.  I do not accommodate appointments outside of these days/hours.
Do you take a deposit?
Yes! A $100 deposit is required to book your appointment. All deposits are non-refundable. This means you do not receive your deposit back if you cancel your appointment.  If you need to reschedule your appointment, your deposit will be carried over if you provide at least 3 days notice.  In the (rare) event that I request to reschedule your appointment, the deposit will always carry over.
What Kind of work are you looking to book?
I am always accepting all booking requests for my own flash! For custom work, I'm looking for concepts that fit my style best. I only tattoo in black and gray, in an illustrative realism style.  

I do not do traditional style tattoos or abstract tattoos.
